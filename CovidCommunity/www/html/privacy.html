Privacy Policy

Our contact tracing solution takes your personal data and privacy very seriously. This page is meant to inform you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Location Data

We collect geolocation and movement data as an opt-out feature to update our system-wide database while your app is running as a background process. This is done to keep our safety and community reports as accurate as possible, allowing us to keep up with potential contact between users and high-risk factors. Any health records you submit through the app are used to validate your current health status, and any extraneous information associated with your records are not saved. We recommend you only submit test results and no documents containing sensitive information.

We do not disclose your data to anyone, period. All data is anonymized and stored for at most 30 days to account for the average viral infectious period, and all stored data is deleted after the 30 day period.
